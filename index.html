Extended Bio - Sandra Owings

With a degree in Travel and Tourism she made her way from Upstate New York to Western Tennessee while working as a hotel clerk, an airline reservations manager, and for many years now an agent and travel agency manger. Sandra also holds accreditation with many islands in the Caribbean, resorts and tourist boards both in the U.S. and abroad.

Travels include a Trans-Atlantic voyage with visits to Italy, Spain and Bermuda; to Seattle and Vancouver, Victoria and Alaska. Trips along the East Coast, skiing in the West, and to street festivals in New Orleans, Destin and the Southern states. With a love for outdoor activities, fishing, photography and her dogs, she also looks forward to spending time with family and friends and smiles as traveling is always on her mind.

Whether you are looking for wildlife sightings, cultural insights, culinary lessons and wine tastings, luxury or moderate travel, or perhaps a once-in-a lifetime experience she can assist with your travel planning needs. Sandra can assist in finding your tour guide, cruise director, resort and butler staff, private yacht or jet.

Extended Bio - Lisa Van Hersh

Hi, my name is Lauren (or as most of my friends call me...Lemmy!) and I am huge supporter of literature, history and adventures! When you mix all of those together you end up with a great travel partner because I love learning about a place before I head there and always throw in local flavor and unique activities.

I was fortunate enough to travel out of the country (to Brazil...TWICE) at a young age and this sparked a light in me that I have never let fade. Since then, I have been to Canada, Puerto Rico, Jamaica, Alaska, Montana and have fell in love with cities like Sacramento, San Francisco, Boston, and Chicago. In the fall of 2017, I organized a New England Coast road trip for my Mom and I; we visited 3 states and 7 cities!

While Instagramming, I started getting constant feedback from friends on how they would love for me to plan them a trip. Then suddenly, I was joining the amazing team at All Things Travel in January of 2018! I am working with a group of women who have made their mark all over this world, literally, and I am so blessed to be able to learn and grow with them.

So let’s figure out today where you can leave your mark. Let’s plan that trip to that city that has all been on your list or that country that inspired you years ago. It’s time to travel! Lemmy be your travel agent!​
