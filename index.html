Let's Go!

There are so many vacation options. We are here to help! At All Things Travel we will be with you every step of the way. Your vacation is something precious and important to you and we want you to have the best experience possible.

Extended Bio - Sandra Owings

Sandra has enjoyed travel all her life. Her interests were sparked by stories, photos and souvenirs from family who traveled the world. With over 30 years in the industry she has been blessed with the friendship of extraordinary people, remarkable experiences and a continued passion for all things travel.

Starting out with a degree in Travel and Tourism she has worked as a hotel clerk, an airline reservations manager, and for many years now travel advisor and agency manger

Travels include a Trans-Atlantic voyage with visits to Italy, Spain and Bermuda; to Seattle and Vancouver, Victoria and Alaska. Trips along the East Coast, skiing in the West, and to street festivals in New Orleans, Destin and the Southern states. Costa Rica and Hawaii were highlights as well as both Mexican coasts, Jamaica and The Dominican Republic. In her free time Sandra enjoys spending time with her husband Chris, son Jason and wife Katie, and her extended family. With a love for cruises, all-inclusive Caribbean resorts and outdoor activities such as fishing, photography and playing with her dog she is always looking forward to her next trip.

She is able to assist with your individual and group travel of all types, destination weddings and honeymoons, reunions, corporate incentive travel, romantic trips and meaningful voluntourism experiences. Whether you are looking for wildlife sightings, cultural insights, culinary lessons and wine tastings, luxury or moderate travel, or perhaps a once-in-a lifetime experience she can assist with your travel planning needs. Sandra can assist in finding your tour guide, cruise director, resort and butler staff, private yacht or jet.

Her certifications include: "A-List" of Top Travel Professionals by Travel + Leisure Magazine, certified Master Cruise Counselor, Authorized Disney Specialist, a Ski Specialist, Australia Certified Specialist and Certified Sandals & Beaches Resort Specialist. Sandra also holds accreditation with many islands in the Caribbean, resorts and tourist boards both in the U.S. and abroad.
